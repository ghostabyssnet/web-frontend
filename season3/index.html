We tried. :(

Season 3 has been CANCELLED. It was a project set to recreate the old MOBA experience using oldschool character and item designs, focusing on fun instead of competition.

MOBAs are no longer inherently fun for most people. Our days of laughing at instant one hit kills and absurd health bars were replaced by frustration of being victim of those very things -- all because of "virtual points".



What if we could go back?

What if we were able to play our favourite characters without having them completely toned down or removed by game updates, the hassle of facing unkillable assassin mage necromancer cleric red goats or being forced to tolerate griefers to avoid being banned?



ENTER SEASON 3





The Season 3 project aims to bring us back to the old times. Pick any hero, play it the way you want to, buy and test strange items. It's 2013 again!

  • Zuki

    Accursed and forgotten, Zuki enters the Black Forest seeking an worthy opponent. Her ultimate causes her to lunge around the battlefield, dealing damage while invulnerable.

  • Ring of Hatred

    This item grants ability power, cooldown reduction and an active that deals damage and increases all magic damage the target receives.

  • Corrus

    One of the Palemoon Kingdom's marquesses, Corrus is an notoriously powerful vampire. Using his claws and fangs, his powers heal himself as he damages his foes. However, the marquess can also sacrifice his health to increase his strength.

  • Nature's Cowl

    Season 3 will not only feature items from the 2013 era, but also community favourites! We think it's fun to let you choose by yourself what you're going to use.

Zuki, the Blademistress

Passive - Luna's Curse

Zuki's sword is imbued with vampirism, granting her lifesteal.

Q - Blackslash

Zuki slashes forward, striking her target with physical damage. She can cast it again within few seconds.

W - Parry

Grants base Attack Damage. When activated, Zuki uses her sword to deflect the next basic attack she's struck with, dealing magic damage.

E - Voracity

For a short while, grants a burst of Attack Speed. Dealing damage during this effect grants Movement Speed. This spell's cooldown is refreshed when Zuki kills an hero.

R - Last Shadow

Consumed by hatred, Zuki becomes untargetable and strikes aimlessly, dealing physical damage to nearby enemy heroes. All basic attack modifiers are applied, but successive blows against the same hero deal reduced damage.

Ring of Hatred

+120 Ability Power

+10% Cooldown Reduction

UNIQUE Active: Deals 15% (+5% per 100 Ability Power) of target hero's maximum health in magic damage, then amplify all magic damage they take by 20% for 4 seconds (60 second cooldown).

Corrus, the Butcher

Passive - Blood Armor

The Butcher stores part of the health drained by his abilities in his Blood Armor, which deteriorates while he's not in combat. When Corrus dies, he drains his Blood Armor to revive himself. Grants bonus Attack Damage and Armor based on how full the Blood Armor is.

Q - Crush

Corrus teleports to a target location, dealing physical damage and stunning enemies around him as he arrives.

W - Gluttony and Greed

Passively heals The Butcher on every third attack.
Activate to exchange this effect for additional magic damage in Corrus' basic attacks, in exchange of his health.

E - Knightbane

Corrus brandishes his sword, dealing magic damage to enemies in his path at the cost of his health.

R - Annihilation

The Butcher unsheathes his sword, dealing physical damage around him and gaining additional Attack Speed and range for a few seconds.
