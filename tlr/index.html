Welcome to The Lost Reality,
a completely free and open-source World of Warcraft mod that aims to recreate Azeroth as it should have been.

What is The Lost Reality?

Nullcst

World of Warcraft: The Lost Reality is a study project and a mod that opens up a new chapter to the game. The idea behind it is to reassess everything that has ever been (or could have been) implemented in World of Warcraft, from Vanilla to Retail. Our community decides what gets added, changed or removed.

It is not Vanilla+

The Lost Reality is not a spiritual successor of Vanilla WoW. In fact, its plot begins right after the ending of World of Warcraft: Shadowlands. In the narrative, Zovaal ends up winning and unmakes reality as he sees fit, only for a new one to be created. The game's story begins exactly where Vanilla's does, but with major changes caused by the reality rift. This, however, opens up space for the game to travel in a new direction, away from the one its been for the past fifteen years.

The thought behind the project is simple: "What would I do if I was in charge of World of Warcraft?"

Gameplay-wise, the project completely rebuilds every feature in the game, from classes to entire zones and raids. Every decision considers our community's input and feedback, akin to OSRS. Its story will be relatively similar to World of Warcraft's original storyline, with some major plot points changed. Overall, it aims to bring a familiar, but confusing, experience. Some quests are the same, some had their objective's locations changed, others disappeared as new ones took their place. Some creatures are slightly easier to defeat, others are unsettlingly difficult to even approach. Enemies have new tricks, allies have new ways to help. Prepare for a new challenge!
